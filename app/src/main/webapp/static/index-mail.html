Welcome to Whatstat

How to send a Whatsapp chat to get stats

Send your conversation to whatstat@mail.remip.eu

There is no need to include your medias (5th bullet for the FAQ). Including medias limits to 10 000 lines whereas 40 000 lines can be sent without the medias. Moreover, Whatstat doesnt' care about your medias.

After sending the email, you will right away receive a mail with a link to follow the parsing progress.

Your chat will be given a unique name to access your stats. Consider keeping it as it allows you to quickly access the stats. It only takes time to read a chat, not to compute stats.

How about my privacy and the privacy of the others in the chat ?

Whatstat is not storing the actual content. The dates, who and how many characters only are kept.

Chat is sent from your email (up to you) to Whatstat mail server (25). Stats (without the actual content of your message) are retrieved over plain HTTP.
