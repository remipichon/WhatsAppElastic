Welcome to Whatstat

How to send a Whatsapp chat to obtain meaningful stats?

Send your chat to whatstat@mail.remip.eu. The email's subject can be used to give a name to your chat'stats.

There is no need to include your medias (5th bullet of the FAQ). Including medias limits to 10 000 lines whereas 40 000 lines can be sent without the medias. Moreover, Whatstat doesnt' care about your medias.

After sending the email, you will right away receive a response with a link to follow the parsing progress.

You will also receive an email once the parsing is done.

A link will be given to you to access your stats. Consider keeping it as it allows you to quickly access the stats. It only takes time to read the chat, not to compute the stats.

FAQ: How to export a WhatsApp chat

How about my privacy and the privacy of the others in the chat ?

Whatstat is not storing the actual content but only the dates, who and how many characters per message.

Chat is sent from your email (up to you) to Whatstat mail server (25). Stats (without the actual content of your message) are retrieved over plain HTTP.

In other words, it's not really secure so far. HTTPS will soon be used to ensure your privacy.
